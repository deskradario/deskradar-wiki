DeskRadar

Real-time aircraft tracking on a 64×64 LED matrix display.

DeskRadar is a hardware and software system that polls live ADS-B data from a local dump1090 receiver and renders nearby aircraft tracks on a 64×64 RGB LED matrix. Altitude is mapped to color, historical trails fade over time, and the closest aircraft is highlighted and shown on a 16×2 LCD display. The entire system runs on a Raspberry Pi alongside an Adafruit MatrixPortal S3 that drives the LED matrix.

AI Transparency

The deskradar and deskradar-pi-services Python codebases were written by me, except some math stuff I couldn't figure out. AI was used for bug finding and code review mostly.
