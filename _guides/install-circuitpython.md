## After Installation

After CircuitPython is installed and the MatrixPortal appears as a `CIRCUITPY` drive, copy everything from the `src` directory in the <a href="https://github.com/deskradario/deskradar-matrixportals3" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">deskradar-matrixportals3 repository</a> onto the MatrixPortal.
