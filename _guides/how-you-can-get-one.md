This is the most complete route if you want to understand the whole system and end up with a DeskRadar you can repair or modify later.

You will need the electronics, housing, power parts, Pi software, and MatrixPortal firmware. Start with the <a href="{{ '/hardware/' | relative_url }}">hardware and bill of materials</a>, then follow the <a href="{{ '/guides/construction-instructions/' | relative_url }}">construction instructions</a>.

In short:

1. Obtain the parts from the bill of materials.
2. Print or buy the enclosure files from <a href="https://www.printables.com/model/1704248-deskradar64-full-housing" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">Printables</a> or <a href="https://shop.deskradar.io">Etsy</a>
3. Flash the <a href="{{ '/pi_image/' | relative_url }}">DeskRadar Pi image</a>.
4. Install the <a href="https://github.com/deskradario/deskradar-matrixportals3" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">MatrixPortal firmware</a>.
5. Assemble according to the <a href="{{ '/guides/construction-instructions/' | relative_url }}">construction instructions</a>.
6. Use the <a href="{{ '/guides/initial-setup/' | relative_url }}">initial setup guide</a> to configure your location and display settings.

Instructions on how to connect to an existing ADSB terminal coming soon.
